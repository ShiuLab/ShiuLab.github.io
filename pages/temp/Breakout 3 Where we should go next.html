Other: research questions generated using Chat GPT?
How do plants perceive and respond to changes in their environment at the molecular level, and how can we use this knowledge to improve crop yields and stress tolerance?
How do different plant species and populations adapt to their local environments, and what are the genetic and epigenetic mechanisms that drive these adaptations?
How do plants interact with and respond to other organisms in their environment, including microbes, insects, and other plants?
How can we use computational methods to better understand and predict the impacts of climate change on plant growth, development, and distribution?
How can we use machine learning and artificial intelligence to identify and optimize plant traits for specific applications, such as biofuels, pharmaceuticals, or food production?
How can we use high-throughput sequencing technologies to study the genetics and evolution of plant populations and species, and to identify new genes and pathways involved in plant growth and development? How can we integrate data from multiple sources, including genomic, transcriptomic, proteomic, and metabolomic data, to gain a more comprehensive understanding of plant biology and function?
How can we use computational models to simulate and predict the behavior of complex plant systems, such as photosynthesis, carbon allocation, and nutrient uptake? How can we develop new tools and technologies to manipulate plant genomes and epigenomes, and to engineer novel plant traits for specific applications?
How can we use computational methods to design and optimize plant breeding strategies, including genomic selection and marker-assisted breeding, to improve crop yields and resilience to environmental stresses?
Preparing and training students in explainable AI (set of tools and frameworks to help in understanding and interpreting predictions made by machine learning models)?
